Sardinian points of interest in your WordPress site

## Description

The Sardinia POI plugin retrieves data from a dataset of Sardinian territory and uses it to populate a shortcode for displaying points of interest (POI). Easily integrate POI information into your WordPress site with customizable templates.

### Key Features

- **Data Integration:** Retrieve data from a dataset of Sardinian territory to display points of interest on your WordPress site.
- **Shortcode:** Use a shortcode to easily display points of interest anywhere on your site.
- **Customizable Templates:** Customize the appearance of the displayed points of interest using templates.
- **Filtering Options:** Filter points of interest based on various criteria such as municipality, province, region, etc.
- **Google Maps Integration:** Each point of interest includes a link to its location on Google Maps for enhanced usability.

## Installation

1. Upload the Sardinia POI plugin to your WordPress site.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Configure the plugin settings via the 'Sardinia POI' menu in the WordPress admin dashboard.

## Usage

- Use the `[sardinia_poi_city]` shortcode in your posts or pages to display points of interest.
- Customize the shortcode attributes to filter points of interest based on specific criteria.

Example:
[sardinia_poi_city comune="Cagliari" limit="5"]
This will display up to 5 points of interest in the city of Cagliari.

## Configuration

- Visit the 'Sardinia POI' menu in the WordPress admin dashboard to configure plugin settings.
- Customize the title area and the template for displaying individual points of interest.
